Our Story

How we began our journey to bridge the gender gap in technology across Africa

EmpowerHer was born from a simple observation — too often, women in tech find themselves alone in the room. Our founder recognized this gap and leveraged her passion for teaching to create a space where girls could feel welcome, supported, and empowered in the world of technology.

We believe technology isn't just a career path; it's a powerful tool for change, creativity, and opportunity. Yet, girls remain significantly underrepresented. EmpowerHer exists to change this narrative by making tech accessible, less intimidating, and deeply engaging for young women across Africa.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a movement that's transforming lives and reshaping the tech landscape one girl at a time.

The Problem We're Solving

The challenges facing girls and young women in technology across Africa

Representation Gap

Despite making up half the population, women represent only a fraction of the tech workforce. This gap starts early and widens through education and career paths.

Entry Barriers

Many girls don't know where to start in tech, lack visible role models, or feel intimidated by male-dominated learning environments.

Confidence Deficit

Even when interest exists, girls often doubt their abilities in STEM fields due to societal messaging and lack of support systems.

Limited Resources

Access to quality tech education, mentorship, and hands-on learning opportunities remains unequal, particularly in underserved communities.

Our Solution

How we're creating opportunities and breaking down barriers in technology

EmpowerHer provides a hybrid learning ecosystem specifically designed for girls and young women aged 15-25 (and beyond). We've created a space where technical skills meet community support, where curiosity transforms into confidence, and where beginners become builders.

Our approach is both practical and inspirational — combining hands-on skill development with mentorship from women who've walked the path. We meet each participant where they are, whether they're writing their first line of code or refining advanced skills.

Through EmpowerHer, girls don't just learn tech; they join a community that celebrates their growth, challenges outdated narratives, and prepares them to lead in an increasingly digital world.
